My Story

About Me

Some stories leave a mark — this is one of them

With over 25 years of experience in cross-border market strategy, business opportunity integration, and high-level resource orchestration, Venus specialises in cross-industry collaboration, international project planning, brand globalisation, and strategic engagement between governments and institutions.


In recent years she has focused on providing full-spectrum "go-global" consultancy for enterprises — helping cultural, tourism, technology, and manufacturing brands successfully enter Korea, Europe, and the Middle East. Every chapter of this journey has been shaped by a quiet conviction: that meaningful work, done with integrity, becomes its own legend.
