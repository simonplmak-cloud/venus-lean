關於我

擁有超過25年跨國市場戰略、商業機遇整合及高層資源運營經驗，專長於行業跨界合作、國際項目規劃、品牌全球化以及政府與機構之間的戰略對接。近年專注於為中國企業提供全鏈路「出海」顧問服務，涵蓋市場研究、品牌定位、跨境合作、國際公關及合規管理，協助多個文化、旅遊、科技與製造業品牌成功進入韓國、歐洲及中東市場。她的每一步，都在書寫屬於自己的傳奇。

About Me

With over 25 years of experience in cross-border market strategy, business opportunity integration, and high-level resource orchestration, I specialise in cross-industry collaboration, international project planning, brand globalisation, and strategic engagement between governments and institutions. In recent years I have focused on providing full-spectrum "go-global" consultancy for Chinese enterprises — helping cultural, tourism, technology, and manufacturing brands successfully enter Korea, Europe, and the Middle East. Every chapter of this journey has been shaped by a quiet conviction: that meaningful work, done with integrity, becomes its own legend.
